PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: February 11, 2026
Last Updated: February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Market Track ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") processes information when you use our mobile application.

1. INFORMATION WE PROCESS

1.1 Local Data

All user-generated content (watchlists, portfolios, preferences, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers to store or access this data. This information remains on your device under your control.

Device Backups: If you have enabled automatic backups on your device (such as iCloud or Google Drive), your locally stored app data may be included in those backups. These backup services are governed by the privacy policies of your device manufacturer or operating system provider. You can manage backup settings through your device settings.

1.2 Analytics and Diagnostics

We use third-party analytics and diagnostics services (Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics) to collect technical and usage data for improving app performance, functionality, and stability.

Information processed may include:

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • App version and configuration
  • Pseudonymous device identifiers
  • Usage events and interaction data
  • Crash logs and performance metrics
  • IP address (processed by our analytics providers)
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Language and device settings

This data is pseudonymous. We do not use this data to directly identify you.

1.3 Third-Party Market Data Providers

The App retrieves financial market data from third-party providers. When you use the App, technical information (including IP address and request metadata) may be transmitted to these providers to fulfill data requests. These providers operate under their own privacy policies and are responsible for their own data processing practices.

1.4 Permissions

The App requires the following permission:

  • INTERNET — Required to retrieve market data and enable core functionality

The App does not request access to camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, storage, or other sensitive device features beyond what is necessary for basic functionality.

1.5 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require or collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • User accounts or registration data
  • Payment information
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Contacts, photos, or files

The App does not require registration.

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Retrieve and display financial market data
  • Monitor performance and stability
  • Analyze aggregated usage patterns to improve the App
  • Detect, prevent, and resolve technical issues
  • Maintain security and integrity of the App

Analytics and diagnostic data are used solely for improving app performance and functionality.

3. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland, processing of analytics and diagnostic data is based on legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR), namely:

  • Ensuring stability and security of the App
  • Improving functionality and user experience
  • Diagnosing and resolving technical issues

We have assessed that such processing is proportionate, limited in scope, and does not override users’ fundamental rights and freedoms.

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests. You may exercise this right by contacting us. You may also limit certain data processing through your device settings where available.

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

4.1 Service Providers

We use third-party service providers (including Google Firebase) to process analytics and diagnostic data on our behalf. These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable data protection agreements.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Firebase Privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Market Data Requests

Technical data necessary to retrieve financial market information is transmitted to market data providers to fulfill your requests within the App.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by applicable law, legal process, court order, or governmental authority.

4.4 Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell personal information as defined under applicable data protection laws.

5. DATA SECURITY AND INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

We take reasonable technical measures to protect data processed through the App.

Portfolio and watchlist data remain stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers for storing user portfolio data.

Certain technical data is processed by third-party service providers (such as Firebase). These providers may process data outside your country of residence, in accordance with applicable data protection laws and appropriate safeguards.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. DATA RETENTION

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the App or clear app data
  • Analytics and Diagnostics Data: Retained according to the policies of the respective service providers

You may reset app data by clearing storage or uninstalling the App.
